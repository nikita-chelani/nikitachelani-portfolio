Virtual Queuing System for Ration Card (April’20 – May’21)

The Virtual Ration Card System is an app to be used by government stores and smalls store retailer to improve the efficiency of time-consuming, maintaining the social distance, and reduce corruption . The App provides applying for ration card at home, choose a location nearby and buy groceries, allocate a token through QR Code and Dashboard helps you to inform about your queue in line and do the hassle-free payment via cash. The Admin keeps the update it by scanning the QR codeThe product to be developed has interactions with the users: Customers, Below Poverty Line People who are the willing to buy from their stores.
